About me

Welcome to my website! I’m a web developer experienced in Ruby, Rails, JavaScript, and Backbone with a love of language, technology, and media. I have a background in publishing, classics, and music, and one of my favorite things about the web is how it can bring all those things together in endless variations.

I’m always looking for projects that align with my interests. If you’re working on something you think I’d enjoy and you’re looking for a developer, a collaborator, or just a fellow brainstormer, send me a message!
